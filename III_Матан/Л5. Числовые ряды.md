$$\sum_{k=1}^n \frac{1}{(n+1)n} = \sum_{k=1}^n \frac{(k+1)-k}{(k+1)k} = \sum_{k=1}^n \frac{1}{k} - \frac{1}{k+1} = \sum_{k=1}^n \frac{1}{k} + \sum_{m=2}^{n+1} \frac{1}{m} = 1 - \frac{1}{n+1} = 1 - \frac{1}{n+1}$$
